CpG methylation

CpG methylation is an epigenetic modification on DNA. At the molecular level, CpG methylation involves the addition of a methyl (CH3) group on the C5 atom of cytosine, yeilding 5-methylcytosine (5mC). Although the change is very small, but cumulative effect is profound, for example X-chromosome inactivation. Recent work have suggested impact of methylation on protein My research work has revealed insights into the methyl-induced structural deformations and its applications protein-DNA interactions.
